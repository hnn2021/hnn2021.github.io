Welcome to my portfolio website!!

On this website, you are going to know more about me, my accomplishments and personal reflections during my study in the Learning Technology and Design program at the University of Missouri-Columbia.

This website is used not only to showcase my personality and attributes, but also to exhibit my skills, knowledge, and experiences of using different technology tools and instructional design principles and techniques to create effective and relevant learning experiences for learners. You will see several projects I created in different courses and how I integrated them into my own classroom to support students learning.

This website is also a place where I share my self-reflections, aspirations, and goals to examine and motivate myself. It is where I dive deeper into my own learning and evaluate my professional growth.

My study in the program was a great learning journey for me, and I am glad to share it with you here…
